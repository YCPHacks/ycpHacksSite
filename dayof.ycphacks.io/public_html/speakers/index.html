Acception and Adoption of Electric Vehicles
Tim Klenner is a study-abroad student from Germany who is working with Professor Hake on an independent study project regarding the acceptance and adoption of electric vehicles. As part of Tim's course work, he has put together an anonymous Google survey. Please take a few minutes to complete the survey, if you have the time. This completely anonymous survey is a collection of questions about the acceptance and adoption of electric vehicles, with a strong emphasis on battery-powered vehicles. It should take no longer than 5 minutes to complete the survey. Thank you for your time.
